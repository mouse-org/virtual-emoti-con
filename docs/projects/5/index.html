“Heroes of COVID-19”

An Art & Social Action Project

By Jessilee, Karen

Middle School Students from Mouse

About this project:

To combat COVID-19, we have been told to “stay inside”. But, in order for us to have food, to move around the city, get our mail, have our city be clean and safe, and to be taken care of when we get sick, there needs to be brave workers out there. They are putting their lives on the line for us and they are HEROES. These portraits honor health care workers and everyday heroes like MTA workers.
