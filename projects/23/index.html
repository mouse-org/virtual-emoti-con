About this project:

People all throughout life have moments when they go through stress and that can be be bad if it prolongs. That is what my project tackles or brings awareness to. To help people my project instructs that you get a pet because they can help you reduce your stress and add some love too. At first there was going to a can only. But then I decided that I should add more than one pets like a dog and a bird as well that move on their own. This is done with a function that tells it to move a certain length before turning back and moving again.
