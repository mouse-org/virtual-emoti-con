“GKHS Dropping Knowledge V1.4”

A Social Impact Game

By Jonathan “SkyLegend”, Joshua “RedLegend”, Katie “KatieCat14”, Patricia “PattieCat”, Kevin “KevCat”

High School Students from Mouse

About this project:

This original game was created collaboratively by the GKHS STEMClub and modified recently to add different backgrounds and sounds for different levels. The students created a “Catch” game that allows the GKHS Wolves – our school logo- to catch and eat school supplies (books, bookbags, pencils) while avoiding eating students and aliens!

It’s a super fun game with arcade-like background music.

    Interesting concept, would love to see more about your prototyping process. Nice design between levels and background music, and the game is quite challenging to beat!

  • Amanda L Perales 👏 Committee Member at Emoti-Con — Emoti-Con Judge

    This game is extremely entertaining. I love the fun background music and sound effects. I suggest making the wolf a little bit smaller and tweaking the parameters a bit or using just the wolf's head.   • Eric Lewis 👏 Engineer Of Things at Power Home Remodeling — Emoti-Con Judge
